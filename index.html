About Me

Hey! I'm İlker Erkek. a passionate computer science graduate hailing from the beautiful city of İzmir, Turkey, I proudly hold my degree from Dokuz Eylül University.

My journey in the realm of technology has been exhilarating, fueled by my keen interest in a multitude of domains. From Data Science and Software Engineering to ML Engineering, Data Engineering, MLOps, and System Architecture, I find joy and purpose in exploring every facet of data.

My Projects and Work

Some of my public projects and works.

Immediate Urgent Information

A visionary mobile application project designed to swiftly inform users during urgent situations. Using the keywords in Turkish and device's microphone, users receive the urgent information quickly. Our cutting-edge solution employs a Convolutional Neural Network, utilizing spectrograms to detect keywords through frequency spectrum analysis. Remarkably, the model is seamlessly hosted on the mobile device using the prowess of Tensorflow.js, ensuring efficiency and accessibility.

Dijkstra's Maze

An engaging Unity simulation for pathfinding algorithms that goes beyond mere visualization, gamifying the experience for enhanced interactivity. Algorithms traverse the grid, revealing their intricacies in a captivating and interactive manner. Lets the user immerse in the dynamic and user-defined environment, gaining a deeper understanding of pathfinding concepts while enjoying the gamified elements.

Digit Recognizer App

Digit recognizing website powered by the Flask framework, hosts the website seamlessly, providing an intuitive interface with a free canvas to draw which users can showcase their creativity. The magic happens as the model steps in, utilizing augmented data to train and refine its predictive abilities. This approach ensures the model can adeptly recognize an array of inputs from a diverse user base.

Basic Graphics Engine

A basic graphics engine, crafted in C++ better grasp computer rendering. application possesses the prowess to render ".obj" files, the fundamental file format commonly utilized in Blender software. The object gets rendered on the screen as basic triangles on the screen, showcasing the building blocks of computer-generated imagery.

Titanic Survival Prediction(Kaggle Competition)

With data analysis and the power of machine learning, this project aimed to unravel patterns and insights from the available dataset. Through the lens of a Linear Classifier, I sought to anticipate who survived this harrowing maritime disaster.
